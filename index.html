The Mission

God has opened an incredible door

Hi, I'm Tate Singleton. I've been invited by the International Mission Board (IMB) to serve alongside the mission God is doing in Japan this summer.

For two months, I'll work alongside IMB partners on the ground, helping point the people of Japan to Christ. I'll be based in Tokyo at the University of Tokyo (Todai), partnering with Local Churches to build relationships with university students through language exchange, summer events, and everyday gospel conversations.
